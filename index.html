About

Who Am I

I’m all about creating seamless and engaging experiences. My career has evolved from administration to operations management and product development, where I focus on maintaining the integrity and adaptability of the systems I work with. I’m passionate about thoughtful design that respects and meets diverse user needs, ensuring accessibility for everyone on their own terms. With a background in behavioral sciences, history, and an MBA, I bring a unique perspective on how societies shape and are shaped by their contexts. This insight helps me design products that are not only functional and intuitive but also empathetic, honoring the rich stories and cultures that influence us. Travel and exploration continually inspire me, deepening my appreciation for the diverse ways people interact with their world. Whether you’re here to explore my projects, read my blog, or connect, I’m excited to collaborate and create something amazing together!

  • Proficient in Product Development with a focus on accessibility and thoughtful design.
  • Skilled in Project Management, Communication, and Cultural Competency.
